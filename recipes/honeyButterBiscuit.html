Honey Butter Biscuit

Honey Butter Biscuits recipe makes an easy drop biscuit with a touch of sweetness from a honey glaze. Baked biscuits are tender and buttery just like you want from a homemade biscuits recipe. If under 15 minutes you can have this special dinner treat that will elevate any meal.

Why You’ll Love This Drop Biscuit Recipe

  • Simple ingredients.You won’t need anything surprising or extra for these easy drop biscuits. You probably already have all of these ingredients in your kitchen.
  • Easy to mix. Mix the dry ingredients together and then cut the butter into the flour. There’s no rolling and stamping with these homemade honey butter biscuits.
  • Bakes in 10-12 minutes. You’ll have hot glazed biscuits on the table in no time at all.

Ingredients for the Honey Butter Biscuits Recipe

  1. All-Purpose Flour. Bleached or unbleached all-purpose flour both work just fine.
  2. Sugar. White table sugar like you mix into your tea.
  3. Baking Powder. Fresh baking powder is a must for making homemade biscuits. Find out if your baking powder is fresh using the instructions in my Baking Basics Guide.
  4. Salt. Kosher table salt or fine sea salt are my favorites.
  5. Cream of Tartar. Acts as a leavening agent in baked goods.
  6. Butter. I use real unsalted butter. If you use salted butter eliminate the additional salt called for in this recipe.
  7. Buttermilk. We get the best results with the full-fat version gives our biscuits flavor and a nicely moist spring.

Step by Step
The Biscuits

  • In a large mixing bowl, add the flour,sugar,baking powder,cream of tartar, vanilla powder, and salt. Whisk to continue
  • Add the butter and cut into the dry ingredients, until the mixture resembles cornmeal
  • Add the milk.Mix lightly until the mixture comes together
  • Kenad about 15 times, to flatten the butter pieces and strengthen the dough a little. Do not Overknead
  • Scoop into a greased tray lined with baking paper

The Honey Butter Glaze

  • Make the glaze. In a saucepan over medium heat,add the butter,honey and malt syrup if using.
  • Bring the mixture to a boil, and remove from heat.

Baking
  • Preheat the over to 230 degrees C (fan 320 degrees C)
  • Glaze the biscuits with the honey butter mixture
  • Bake in the oven and bake, until golden brown. 10 to 12 minutes
  • Remove from the oven.Brush the baked biscuits with another layer of glaze
  • Let cool on a wire rack.Serve warm
